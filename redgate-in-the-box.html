Redgate in the box with Visual Studio (2017)

In March 2017 Redgate partnered to with Microsoft to include three Redgate products in the box with Visual Studio. The aim was to expand Visual Studio’s database development functionality and widen Redgate’s reach by providing a number of free tools to Visual Studio Enterprise users.

Problem

I was tasked with building a joined-up Redgate experience within Visual Studio.

  • Project under a very challenging timeline
  • Integrate the Redgate licensing model into Visual Studio
  • Design experiences which give new users a good initial impression of Redgate’s products – for example by allowing users to use the products without immediately having to sign in.
  • Work across four different product teams who were building the component parts.
